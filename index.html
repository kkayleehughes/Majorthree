My Journey to UCF

By: Kaylee Hughes

This is my journey to UCF, below is a series of photos that show sections of my life leading up to UCF and how I got to where I am

Hover over the images to get a description of what the image represents!

1.) In 2020 when Covid hit, I moved to Orlando where I started online school with FLVS to finish out my senior year. The goal was to finish my senior year in a semester instead of a year to start college early.
2.) Then, on a random Thursday in December of 2020 I submitted my last assignment and offically Graduated highschool. Since it was online I did not have a graduation and so I did not get to celebrate graduating highschool.
3.) Spring of 2021, I started at Valencia College with intent to do their transfer program with UCF at this time classes were still online. My dad works at Valencia so I was able to get a few free credits a semester so I took the opprotunity and worked hard.
4.) In December of 2022, I graduated with my AA from Valencia College! I was able to have a graduation which made up for not having on in highschool. This was the final step to getting to UCF!
5.) After many days of trying to transfer everything over, I am now at UCF and on track to graduate Spring of 2025 with a major in Digital Media and Platform Development.

My future plan is once I gradute from UCF to enter the workforce. I do not have a specific job I would like to pursue, so as of now I have decided to be open to any opprotunity that comes my way!
